How It Works

The Mining Process

  • Recharge your CryptoJet balance via bank transfer or USDT (BEP20 / TRC20)
  • Purchase any miner tier from the Miner tab and tap Start
  • Your miner runs its full cycle — 24 hours for S-900/S-1200, 48 hours for S-244/S-500
  • At cycle end, your exact daily earnings are automatically credited to your balance
  • Restart the cycle as many times as you want — earnings compound over your plan duration

⚡ Recharge

Fund your account via Opay bank transfer or USDT. Deposits reflect in 3–20 minutes after verification.

🏦 Withdraw

Cash out to any Nigerian bank account or USDT wallet. Processed within 48 hours Mon–Fri. 10% fee applies.

🔐 Security

Withdrawal password protection, Firebase Auth, and admin-verified transactions keep your balance safe.

📲 Access

Fully mobile-first web app. No download needed — runs in any browser on any device, anywhere.

Referral System

Earn From Your Team

When people you refer complete mining cycles, a percentage goes straight to your Team Income balance — 3 levels deep.

Level 1 — Direct referrals 10%
Level 2 — Their referrals 4%
Level 3 — Next level 2%

Team income is separate from your main balance and can be used to purchase miners or withdrawn directly.
